# 🌿 NDVI Vegetation Dynamics Analysis and Forecasting for Aligarh District

## Overview

This repository contains the research work conducted as part of my M.Sc. Statistics dissertation at Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh.

The study investigates long-term vegetation dynamics in Aligarh District, Uttar Pradesh using MODIS satellite-derived NDVI data. Geospatial processing was performed through Google Earth Engine, while statistical modelling and forecasting were carried out using R.

---

## Dissertation Title

**Long-Term NDVI Trend Analysis and Forecasting for Aligarh District, Uttar Pradesh Using MODIS Satellite Data, Google Earth Engine, and Bayesian Time Series Modelling**

---

## Research Objectives

- Assess long-term vegetation trends in Aligarh District.
- Generate monthly and annual NDVI series from MODIS data.
- Examine temporal changes in vegetation dynamics.
- Apply time series models for NDVI forecasting.
- Validate forecast performance using observed data.
- Support environmental monitoring through geospatial analytics.

---

## Study Area

**Aligarh District, Uttar Pradesh, India**

The study focuses on analysing vegetation variability and long-term ecological patterns across the district using remotely sensed satellite observations.
